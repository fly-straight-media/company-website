Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2021

This Privacy Policy explains how Fly Straight Media Kft. ("Fly Straight Media", "we" or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information while you use any of our apps, websites and/or services ("Services").

What data do we collect and how do we use it?

When you use our Services, we automatically collect information about the device ("Device") you use to access our Services and how you interact with our Services. This data includes device specific identifiers and information such as IP address, cookie information, mobile device and advertising identifiers, browser version, operating system type and version, mobile network information, device settings, and software data. We process this data for analytical purposes on the basis of our legitimate interests, so we can improve the quality of our Services.

Advertising

To help us keep our Services running, we show ads to our users through the help of our third-party advertising partners ("Ad Partners"). Our Ad Partners might collect information about you to serve you ads that you might find more interesting ("Targeted Ads"). This information includes device specific identifiers and advertising identifiers. If you have location services enabled and you have allowed access to them, our Ad Partners might also collect your location information. If you do not want to receive Targeted Ads on your mobile Device, your mobile Device may give you the option to opt-out of receiving Targeted Ads on all apps on your Device. On Android Devices, you may opt-out by enabling the "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting. On iOS Devices, you may opt-out by enabling the "Limit Ad Tracking" setting. Please note that if you opt-out of receiving Targeted Ads, you will still receive contextual ads, which are non-targeted ads related to the content of the Game you are playing.

How long do we keep your data?

We will only keep your data as long as it’s necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for. Data collected for analytical purposes may be stored for an indefinite time, as we often compare freshly collected data with data collected in the past to help us measure the impact of our decisions.

How do we keep your data secure?

We do our best to keep your information secure and apply appropriate measures. However, as with most of your data stored online, you should not expect that it will always remain secure, even though it is in our best interest to keep it so.

Cross-border data transfers

Sharing your information with third-parties often involves transferring your data to countries outside of the European Economic Area ("EEA"). We employ appropriate safeguards to permit transferring your data outside the EEA binding our partners to protect the privacy and security of your data just like we do.

Your rights

It is in our best interest to inform you about your rights regarding the information we collect from you. Every user is entitled to the following rights:

  • The right to access - You have the right to request copies of the data we have collected on you. We might charge a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification - You have the right to request us to correct any information we have on you that you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request us to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure - You have the right to request us to erasure any data we collected on you, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to processing your data under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability - You have the right to request us to transfer the data that we collected about you to another organisation, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you.
